Introduction to Digital Transformation with Siemens

Project Info:
During the final week of the course, you will learn about each step in the product lifecycle. You will examine how digital transformation occurs during product ideation, realization, and utilization. At the end of the week, you will examine how digital transformation operates in the Consumer Produces and Marine industries. At the end of this week is also our final course exam. This will cover all instructional information from weeks four through five. Learning Objectives

Identify techniques and applications of digital solutions across the product lifecycle. Explain the roles and importance of ideation, realization, and utilization within a product lifecycle. Describe the concepts of a product lifecycle. Evaluate applications of digital transformation to the Consumer Products industry. Identify key issues and trends in the Consumer Products industry. Evaluate applications of digital transformation to the Marine industry. Identify key issues and trends in the Marine industry.
